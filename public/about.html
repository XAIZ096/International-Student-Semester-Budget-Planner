How to Use This App

  1. Create budget categories for one semester, such as rent, tuition, food, travel, and transportation.
  2. Add transactions as income or expenses. Use a currency label such as USD, CNY, or SGD.
  3. Enter a manual exchange rate to USD when the transaction is not in USD.
  4. Open the dashboard to see total income, total expenses, balance, and remaining budget by category.
  5. Edit or delete records when your plans change.
